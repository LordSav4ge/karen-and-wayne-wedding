Our Story

How We Met

Some love stories begin with a grand gesture, but ours began with a simple hello that changed everything. We connected the way any millennials would, trying to circumvent the horrors of online dating, only to realize our paths first crossed as teenagers at a youth camp in 2004. Life took us on separate journeys, but something kept pulling us back to each other. From the moment we reconnected, we knew this was the connection we’d been searching for all along.

The Journey

Through adventures near and far, quiet evenings and spontaneous trips, we’ve built a life filled with laughter, love, and countless memories. Every moment together has been a reminder of how lucky we are to have found each other.

Forever Starts Now

And now, surrounded by the people we love most, we’re ready to make it official. We can’t wait to celebrate this next chapter with you and begin our forever together.
